🎭 Gujarat to Kolkata: Cultural Capital Journey

📅 Published: March 10, 2024 | ✍️ By Mr Amar Jankar | 🚗 Lankadhish Cab Service

🎨 Cultural Odyssey: 1400km journey to India's cultural capital! Experience Bengali art, literature, music, and the intellectual heritage of Kolkata.

🗺️ Cultural Route

  • Distance: 1,400 km via Bhubaneswar
  • Travel Time: 24-26 hours (3-day journey)
  • Heritage Route: NH19 → NH16
  • Cultural Stops: Santiniketan, Mayurbhanj

🏛️ Cultural Landmarks

  • Victoria Memorial: British colonial architecture
  • Indian Museum: Oldest museum in India
  • Howrah Bridge: Iconic steel cantilever bridge
  • Dakshineswar Temple: Ramakrishna's spiritual center

🎭 Art & Literature

  • Rabindra Bharati: Tagore's legacy
  • Academy of Fine Arts: Art exhibitions
  • College Street: Book lovers' paradise
  • Kalighat Paintings: Traditional folk art

🍽️ Bengali Cuisine

  • Fish Curry: Bengali staple dish
  • Rosogolla: Famous sweet delicacy
  • Mishti Doi: Sweet yogurt
  • Adda Culture: Tea and intellectual discussions

🎉 Festivals & Culture

  • Durga Puja: Grand festival celebration
  • Kali Puja: Divine mother worship
  • Poila Boishakh: Bengali New Year
  • Cultural Programs: Music, dance, theater
