🌴 Tropical Paradise: 1300km journey to God's Own Country! Experience backwaters, spice plantations, hill stations, and Kerala's natural beauty.

🗺️ Paradise Route

  • Distance: 1,300 km via Goa-Karnataka
  • Travel Time: 22-24 hours (3-day journey)
  • Coastal Route: NH66 → NH544
  • Scenic Stops: Goa beaches, Western Ghats

🛶 Backwater Experiences

  • Alleppey: Houseboat cruises
  • Kumarakom: Bird sanctuary, luxury resorts
  • Kollam: Ashtamudi Lake backwaters
  • Kottayam: Rubber plantations, churches

🏔️ Hill Stations

  • Munnar: Tea plantations, cool climate
  • Thekkady: Spice gardens, wildlife
  • Wayanad: Coffee estates, waterfalls
  • Idukki: Dam, mountain views

🌶️ Spice & Cuisine

  • Spice Plantations: Cardamom, pepper, cinnamon
  • Kerala Cuisine: Fish curry, appam, puttu
  • Coconut Delights: Fresh coconut water
  • Ayurvedic Treatments: Traditional wellness
