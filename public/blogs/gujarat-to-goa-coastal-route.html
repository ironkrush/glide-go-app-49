🗺️ Best Coastal Route: Gujarat to Goa

Scenic Highway Route:

Ahmedabad → Surat → Mumbai → Ratnagiri → Sindhudurg → Goa

  • Total Distance: 600 km
  • Travel Time: 10-12 hours (with scenic stops)
  • Best Season: November to February (perfect weather)
  • Highway: NH48 → NH66 (Coastal Highway)
  • Scenic Factor: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Breathtaking coastal views)

🏖️ Must-Visit Coastal Stops

🌅 Daman & Diu (Gujarat Border)

Distance from Ahmedabad: 180 km

  • Highlights: Portuguese architecture, Diu Fort, pristine beaches
  • Best Time: Early morning for sunrise views
  • Duration: 2-3 hours stop recommended

🏰 Alibaug (Maharashtra)

Distance from Mumbai: 95 km

  • Highlights: Kolaba Fort, clean beaches, water sports
  • Perfect for: Lunch break and beach walk
  • Local Specialty: Fresh seafood and coconut water

🐚 Ratnagiri (Konkan Coast)

Midway Point: Perfect overnight stop

  • Famous for: Alphonso mangoes, Ganpatipule beach
  • Accommodation: Beach resorts and budget hotels
  • Activities: Temple visit, beach photography

🏖️ Sindhudurg (Maharashtra-Goa Border)

Last Maharashtra Stop: 50 km before Goa

  • Highlights: Sindhudurg Fort, pristine beaches
  • Perfect for: Final break before Goa entry
  • Photography: Stunning sunset views

🌊 Coastal Travel Tips

  • Weather: Carry light cotton clothes and sunscreen
  • Monsoon Alert: Avoid June-September (heavy rains)
  • Beach Essentials: Swimwear, towels, waterproof bags
  • Food Safety: Stick to reputed restaurants for seafood
  • Photography: Best light during golden hours
  • Cash: Carry sufficient cash for beach shacks and tolls
  • Permits: No special permits needed for coastal route

🦀 Coastal Cuisine Highlights

  • Gujarati Coast: Dhokla, Khandvi, fresh coconut water
  • Konkan Delights: Fish curry, sol kadhi, modak
  • Goan Specialties: Fish thali, bebinca, feni
  • Seafood: Fresh catch at every coastal stop
