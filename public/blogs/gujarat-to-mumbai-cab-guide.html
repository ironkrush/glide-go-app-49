Scenic highway route from Gujarat to Mumbai with major stops

1. Vadodara (100 km from Ahmedabad)

Perfect for breakfast stop. Visit Laxmi Vilas Palace if time permits. Multiple restaurants and fuel stations available. Vadodara is known as the cultural capital of Gujarat and offers excellent dining options for travelers heading to Mumbai.

2. Surat (150 km from Vadodara)

Famous for textiles and diamonds. Great lunch options and shopping opportunities. The city offers excellent Gujarati thali restaurants. Surat is a major commercial hub and provides the best mid-journey break for Gujarat to Mumbai cab travelers.

3. Vapi (Border town)

Last major stop in Gujarat. Good place for final break before entering Maharashtra. Multiple hotels and restaurants available. Vapi marks the Gujarat-Maharashtra border and is the final checkpoint before entering Mumbai metropolitan region.
