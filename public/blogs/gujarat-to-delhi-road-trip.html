Recommended Highway Route:

Ahmedabad → Udaipur → Ajmer → Jaipur → Gurgaon → Delhi

  • Travel Time: 12-14 hours (with breaks)
  • Best Season: October to March (pleasant weather)
  • Highway: NH48 → NH58 → NH8

🏰 Major Attractions & Stops

🌅 Day 1: Ahmedabad to Udaipur (260 km - 4 hours)

Morning Departure (6 AM): Start early to reach Udaipur by afternoon

  • Breakfast Stop: Himmatnagar (Highway dhaba)
  • Udaipur Highlights: City Palace, Lake Pichola, Jagdish Temple
  • Overnight: Stay in Udaipur (recommended)

🏛️ Day 2: Udaipur to Jaipur (390 km - 6 hours)

Early Start (7 AM): Long day with historical stops

  • En Route: Chittorgarh Fort (optional 2-hour detour)
  • Lunch Stop: Ajmer (Dargah Sharif visit possible)
  • Evening Arrival: Jaipur - Pink City exploration

🏙️ Day 3: Jaipur to Delhi (300 km - 5 hours)

Comfortable Journey: Shortest leg with smooth highways

  • Morning: Quick Jaipur sightseeing (Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort)
  • Departure: 12 PM for afternoon Delhi arrival
  • Route: Jaipur → Gurgaon → Delhi

📋 Essential Travel Tips

  • Documentation: Carry Aadhaar, driving license, and vehicle papers
  • Cash: Keep ₹5,000+ for tolls, food, and emergencies
  • Weather Check: Rajasthan can be extremely hot in summer
  • Hotel Booking: Pre-book accommodations in Udaipur and Jaipur
  • Emergency Kit: First aid, medicines, phone chargers
  • Food: Try local Rajasthani cuisine but carry safe snacks
