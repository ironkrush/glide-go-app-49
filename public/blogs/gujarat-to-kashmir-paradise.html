🗺️ Paradise Route

  • Distance: 1,200 km via Delhi-Jammu
  • Travel Time: 20-24 hours (2-day journey)

🌸 Kashmir Highlights

  • Dal Lake: Floating gardens, houseboats
  • Gulmarg: Skiing, cable car rides
  • Pahalgam: Valley of shepherds
  • Sonamarg: Meadow of gold

🌡️ Best Time to Visit

  • Spring (Mar-May): Blooming flowers, pleasant weather
  • Summer (Jun-Aug): Perfect escape from heat
  • Autumn (Sep-Nov): Golden landscapes
  • Winter (Dec-Feb): Snow activities, skiing

🏔️ Adventure Activities

  • Shikara Rides: Dal Lake boat tours

  • Trekking: Mountain trail adventures
